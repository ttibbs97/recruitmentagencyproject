Recruitment agency specialising in exceptional staff

Headquartered in London, we are a recruitment agency that specialises in matching exceptional support staff to top businesses and private individuals globally. Initially set up in 2001 as an administrative recruitment agency with a focus on personal assistant (PA) recruitment, secretarial recruitment and virtual services, we have since expanded our areas of expertise to encompass HR recruitment, financial recruitment, private staff recruitment, hospitality staff recruitment and technology recruitment. Tiger brings a fiercely distinctive approach to hiring staff. As a Certified B Corp, our brand is synonymous with excellence, positivity, integrity, dedication and gravitas and we aim to deliver an exceptional service to our clients and candidates in everything we do. Our expert consultants only work with high-calibre individuals and offer a tailored approach to recruitment in London and beyond. This is what makes us one of the market-leading permanent and temp recruitment agencies in London.
